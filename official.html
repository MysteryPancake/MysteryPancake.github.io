Videos

Since 2012, I've been making videos using Adobe After Effects.

I began with Microsoft PowerPoint, which allows for a surprising amount of advanced visual effects.

Then I switched to Windows Movie Maker, which could not perform visual effects as well as PowerPoint.

Adobe Premiere Pro didn't work on my laptop, so I used After Effects.

I found that it could do more than PowerPoint and Movie Maker combined!
